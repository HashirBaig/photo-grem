About Our Web App

Welcome to our interactive photogrammetry web application, designed for analyzing and visualizing photogrammetric products. Our platform utilizes CloudCompare and ERDAS to create a Digital Surface Model (DSM) and Digital Terrain Model (DTM) from aerial imagery and AHN data, enabling users to gain precise insights into surface and urban changes.

What We Offer

Interactive DSM Visualization

Leveraging ERDAS and CloudCompare's capabilities, our platform visualizes a highly accurate DSM both in 2.5D and 3D formats, making it an essential tool for professionals in urban planning and disaster assessment.

Interactive DTM Visualization

Generated from the DSM using CloudCompare, this DTM provides reliable information regarding the terrain of the study area and is essential for applications such as flood modelling and others

Web-Based Accessibility

No software installation required. Our web app allows users to access and visualise data from anywhere, ensuring convenience and efficiency.

Who Can Benefit

  • Urban planners and city administrators.
  • GIS professionals and remote sensing specialists.
  • Real estate developers and surveyors.
