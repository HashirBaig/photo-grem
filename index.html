About Our Web App

Welcome to our cutting-edge web application, designed to revolutionize how photogrammetric products are analyzed and visualized. Our platform utilizes the powerful Segment Anything Model (SAM) to automatically segment buildings from aerial and satellite imagery, enabling users to gain precise insights into structural and urban changes.

What We Offer

Advanced Building Segmentation

Leveraging SAM’s AI-driven capabilities, our platform delivers highly accurate building segmentation results, making it an essential tool for professionals in urban planning, real estate, and disaster assessment.

Before & After Analysis

With our intuitive slider interface, users can seamlessly compare pre- and post-segmentation imagery, providing a clear and interactive visualization of changes over time.

Web-Based Accessibility

No software installation required. Our web app allows users to access and analyze segmented data from anywhere, ensuring convenience and efficiency.
